Why Should I create a Meal Plan?

Creating a meal plan can seem like a waste of time, or hinder the flow of creativity unleashed when creating meals on the fly but a meal plan does the opposite.
Here are some of the reasons why you should consider creating a meal plan:

1

Save Time

Set up meal preps ahead of time for even a week's worth of meals. If Monday's breakfast is oats, then why not make Monday's breakfast overnight oats?

2

Incorporate Variety

Budget-friendly eating doesn't mean sacrificing variety; try peanut butter porridge and organize themed dinner nights for excitement. Enjoy a variety of meals from Mexican, Chinese, and Mediterranean.

3

Eat Healthier

Eating a juicy burger with fries sounds epic. But switching to healthier alternatives may increase your life span and keep you in an abundance of health wealth.

4

Reduce Food Waste

Cooking on the fly sometimes robs you of the opportunity to prepare a meal with an Ingredient that is trapped in the back of your fridge and sitting on its last legs. With a meal plan, you can seamlessly incorporate Ingredients that are headed for the bin.

5

Save Money

Less food waste equals more money saved.
Pro-tip: Researching meal ideas ahead of time can help you cook meals that do not break the bank.

6

Prevent Impulse Eating

Sometimes we eat not because we are hungry but because we are trying to fill an empty space. Having a meal plan can help us stay motivated to break this habit.
